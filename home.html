About HealthApp

HealthApp is designed with your well-being in mind. We believe managing your health should be simple, accessible, and stress-free.
Aligned with SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being), our app provides a calming and intuitive interface to help you keep track of your medications and stay organized with your health routines.

We prioritize accessibility and user-friendliness, ensuring that everyone can easily navigate and benefit from our features. Our clean design and calming aesthetic contribute to a positive and supportive user experience.

Key Features

Medicine Tracking

Easily add and manage your list of medications, dosages, and frequencies.

Reminders (Coming Soon)

Set up reminders to ensure you never miss a dose (feature to be implemented in future versions).

Progress Monitoring (Future)

Track your medication adherence and health progress over time (future feature).

Accessible Design

Designed for ease of use by everyone, adhering to accessibility guidelines.
